r220a	"<Bold>r220a Combat Procedure</Bold>
<LineBreak/><LineBreak/>Combat is performed in rounds, and it continues until one side escapes or is wiped out. 
<LineBreak/><LineBreak/>At the start of each round, you decide which of your characters will face each character encountered. After the opponents are matched against each other, all characters on one side strike, and results are applied.  All characters on the other side strike back and results are applied. 
<LineBreak/><LineBreak/>You and your characters may strike first or second each round depending on the event. "
r220b	"<Bold>r220b Selecting Opponents</Bold>
<LineBreak/><LineBreak/>Each character in your party is matched against one encountered character. Each selects a different enemy. 
<LineBreak/><LineBreak/>If one side has extra characters, you decide which the extra face. They can all face one opponent, or each can face a different one provided each character has at least one opponent to face. "
r220c	"<Bold>r220c Strikes</Bold>
<LineBreak/><LineBreak/>When one side strikes, each character on that side strikes once against the opposing character he/she faces. If two or more characters both face an opponent, both strike at him/her. Each strike is resolved separately.
<LineBreak/><LineBreak/>To resolve a strike, take the combat skill of the striker and subtract from it the combat skill of the target character. If the target has better skill, the result is a negative number. Roll two die and add the result of the skill comparison. 
<LineBreak/><LineBreak/>Finally, add or subtract any special modifiers that apply to the total. These special modifiers are:
<LineBreak/><LineBreak/>+2 target has wounds equaling 1/2 or more of his endurance
<LineBreak/>+1 for each target day of starvation
<LineBreak/>-1 striker has one or more wounds
<LineBreak/>-1 striker has wounds equaling 1/2 or more his endurance
<LineBreak/>-1 for each striker day of starvation
<LineBreak/><LineBreak/>Take the total that results and consult the Combat Table
 <InlineUIContainer><Button Name='myButtonR220d' Content='r220d' FontFamily='Courier New'  FontSize='12'></Button></InlineUIContainer>.
<LineBreak/><LineBreak/>If one of the listed numbers has occurred, the target suffers the number of wounds indicated. If a number results that is not listed in the Combat Table, the strike missed and no wounds are inflicted."
